Lumina — Student Productivity Ecosystem
Master Product Specification Prompt


Role: You are a Senior Full-Stack Engineer, UI/UX Designer, and AI Systems Architect with expertise in building production-grade, design-forward web applications.
Objective: Design and build Lumina — a web-based Student Productivity Ecosystem that combines a Smart Academic Calendar, an AI Study Assistant, a Digital Library, and a Focus Timer into a single, cohesive, beautifully designed platform. The product should feel like Notion meets Linear meets ChatGPT, built specifically for university students.


Part 1 — Product Vision & Core Philosophy
Lumina solves one core problem: students are overwhelmed because their tools are fragmented. Their schedule is in one app, their books in another, their AI assistant in a third. Lumina unifies everything into a single dashboard that is:

Clean but information-rich — every piece of data has a place; nothing is hidden more than one click away
Context-aware — the AI knows your exam dates, your reading progress, and your free time slots
Visually honest — colors, shapes, and animations carry meaning, not decoration

2.2 Subject Color System
Every subject has one assigned color. This color must appear identically in four places: the calendar event block, the book spine/cover tint, the progress ring/bar, and the task tag badge.

Rule: Never use a color for pure decoration. Every color must communicate subject identity.
2.3 Dark Mode — Deep Charcoal, Not Pure Black
The background must be #0B0E14. Pure black (#000000) causes eye strain under prolonged use. The slight warmth of deep charcoal reduces fatigue during late-night study sessions, which is a primary use case for this product.
Apply a subtle noise grain overlay (2–3% opacity SVG filter) over the entire page to add texture and prevent the flat look of pure dark backgrounds.
2.4 Glassmorphism for the AI Panel
The AI Assistant sidebar must use glassmorphism:
cssbackground: rgba(17, 21, 32, 0.75);
backdrop-filter: blur(20px);
-webkit-backdrop-filter: blur(20px);
border-left: 1px solid rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.10);
Add two radial gradient glow spots (one indigo, one sky blue) positioned in the top-right and bottom-left corners of the panel at very low opacity (0.15–0.25). This creates the illusion of a floating, luminous panel separate from the main content.

Part 5 — AI Assistant Sidebar Specification
This sidebar is persistent across all pages. It never disappears.
5.1 Structure (top to bottom)

Header: 32px gradient orb (indigo → sky blue, pulsing glow animation), "StudyGPT" title, green status dot with "Context loaded"
Chat history area: Scrollable, flex-column, gap 12px. AI messages align left (glass bubble), user messages align right (indigo solid bubble)
Quick-action chips row: Horizontal scroll. Each chip is a common query. Examples: "Study plan for today", "Explain integration by parts", "Focus tips", "Summarize chapter"
Message input: Grows vertically up to 100px. Rounded border, indigo glow ring on focus. Send button (indigo, with glow shadow)

5.2 Context Window (how the AI thinks)
Before every API call, the frontend builds a context string and sends it to POST /api/ai/chat. The context must include:
- All of the user's active books (title, current_page, total_pages, subject)
- All upcoming calendar events for the next 7 days (title, type, start_time)
- The user's focus_logs summary for the past 7 days (subject, total_minutes)
- The current date and time
This context is prepended to the user's message before sending to the Anthropic API. The AI therefore always knows the user's schedule, reading status, and study history — it never gives generic advice.
5.3 AI Behavior Rules

Always prioritize exam preparation above all other suggestions
Never suggest a time slot that conflicts with an existing calendar event
When a student says they are tired or unfocused, offer a break strategy first, then a lighter study task
Keep responses concise: maximum 4 sentences or a short structured plan with bullet points
After generating a study plan with time slots, always offer to add it to the calendar
